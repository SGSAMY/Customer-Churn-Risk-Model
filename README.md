## Customer Churn Risk Model

## Overview

This project demonstrates a Python-based customer churn risk model developed for financial services products including:

* CTF
* JISA
* ISA
* LISA

The model uses behavioural analytics and customer engagement indicators to identify customers at risk of disengagement, inactivity, or attrition.

The solution helps organisations proactively target at-risk customers through retention campaigns and personalised engagement strategies.

## Business Problem

Financial services organisations need to identify customers who may:

stop contributing
disengage digitally
ignore campaigns
cancel Direct Debits
reduce product activity
potentially leave

This project analyses customer behavioural and engagement data to calculate churn risk scores and classify customers into risk categories.

## Technologies Used

* Python
* pandas
* matplotlib
* Excel
* Jupyter Notebook

The model uses the following data points:

* Fresco customer segmentation
* Product type
* Direct Debit status
* Failed Direct Debit activity
* Complaint history
* Online login activity
* Email engagement
* Website engagement
* Previous campaign response
* Account value
* Marketing channel
* Model Logic

The churn risk model uses rule-based scoring to identify customers with higher attrition risk.

Customers receive higher churn scores based on:

* failed Direct Debits
* high complaint volumes
* low digital engagement
* low email open rates
* low website activity
* inactivity
* lack of campaign response

The final churn score is categorised into:

* High Risk
* Medium Risk
* Low Risk
